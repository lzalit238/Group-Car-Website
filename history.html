1965 Shelby Mustang GT350 & 1966 Shelby GT350

These cars were the smallest and lightest cars of the GT 350 models. They were built specifically for racing and this model was the champion for three years straight.

1967 Shelby GT350/ GT500

There were plans to introduce a convertible during production but they fell through due to financial and supply problems. One 1967 Fastback was updated to a GT500 with a racing engine producing 650 hp. It was known as the "Super Snake".

1967-1968 Shelby CObra GT350

The 1967 model was heavier than its predecessor because of added features, including a longer hood. It also had new taillights and ten-spoke, fifteen-inch, cast-aluminum rims.
